Camelot is Bad

TL;DR: Think twice before renting a property from or associated with Camelot. They severely disadvantage tenants and systematically seek the boundaries of the tenancy law.

In recent years Camelot has been regularly discredited by serious malpractices in their business operations of renting out properties to - often vulnerable - tenants. Problems range from not paying back deposits after the termination of a lease, charging wrongful service costs to poor or no maintenance of properties.

This overview serves as a warning for people who intend to rent a property from Camelot. As a former tenant, I strongly advise you not to rent a property from them.
